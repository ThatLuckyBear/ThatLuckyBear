## What is VGATWD?
VGATWD or Video Games All The Way Down is a concept for a series (video or written essay) that explains life and the different things we can learn or experience within explained through the lens of a video game concept.

## why?
I think it might help neurodivergent people like me figure things out a bit better. a bit of positive masculinity too as it'll mostly be targeting an audience of young men. 

## Essay Structure/Formula
Generally the structure is:
1) Title introduces the comparison 
-- The "Friend Zone" is a False Door"
2) Introduce the game mechanic 
-- "false doors don't have anything behind them, they don't open. if you glitch to the other side there's an empty void because there's nothing there to discover."
3) introduce the life scenario and connect the two concepts
-- "if a romantic interest says I just want to be friends they have shut the door but it's not a door you can unlock. Its a false door."
4) explain the connection and how it helps make sense of the scenario 
-- "you could change yourself and pretend to be someone you're not but this equates to glitching past the door. once you're in the deception will fall apart and not only will you not get the romance you wanted, you'll pretty likely lose the friendship too."
5) reiterate the point and offer a few pointers on how to handle the situation.
-- "Don't try to force a false door, look for the one that's open. Friendship might be the open door but it might not be possible for you to turn off your feelings. in that case the open door might be to take a break from seeing them for a while so you can process the situation. be honest and upfront and take your time before deciding what the best thing to do is."

## Format, Distribution, and Monetization 
while this makes a great YouTube channel idea it might not be possible to do (for me) for some time so written essays that can be restructured into video scripts later wouldn't be a bad choice. it could also be compiled into a volume with a glossary and index for future sales. YouTube ads wouldn't be ideal and YouTube might be best as a jumping off point to direct readers to a site or other streaming platform.
